Common AC Problems That Require Emergency Repairs

When your AC system stops working suddenly, it could be due to several common issues. Some problems can be repaired quickly, while others may require a replacement.

Here are some common AC issues that may necessitate emergency repairs:

1. AC Not Turning On

If your AC unit refuses to turn on, there could be a variety of issues at play. It might be due to a tripped circuit breaker, a faulty thermostat, or an electrical problem within the system. Comfort Zone HVAC’s technicians will troubleshoot the issue and restore your cooling system in no time.

2. Warm Air Blowing from Vents

If your AC is running but only blowing warm air, the problem could be due to refrigerant leaks, a faulty compressor, or a malfunctioning thermostat. Our technicians will quickly diagnose and repair the issue to restore proper cooling to your home.

3. Strange Noises

Unusual noises, such as banging, hissing, or clanging, can be a sign of a serious problem within your air conditioner. These sounds could indicate that parts of the AC system are failing or that the system is experiencing airflow issues. It’s important to address strange noises immediately to prevent further damage to the unit.

4. Frozen Coils

Frozen coils are a common issue in malfunctioning air conditioners. If the refrigerant level is low or the airflow is blocked, the coils can freeze, preventing the AC from working efficiently. Comfort Zone HVAC can inspect the system, thaw the coils, and resolve the underlying cause of the issue.

5. Water Leaks

An AC unit that is leaking water may have a clogged drainage line or a broken pump. Water leaks can damage your home and other parts of the air conditioning system, so it's important to address the issue quickly. Our technicians will find the source of the leak and make the necessary repairs.
